Most Popular Experience

Gorilla Trekking

Trek through the misty bamboo forests of Bwindi Impenetrable National Park or Mgahinga Gorilla National Park for an intimate encounter with a habituated gorilla family. Spend a magical hour in the presence of these gentle giants, observing their complex social behaviors, playful youngsters, and the awe-inspiring silverback patriarch.

This is a life-changing experience that supports vital conservation efforts and local communities. Uganda is one of only three countries where you can see mountain gorillas in the wild.

Duration 4-8 hours
Difficulty Moderate to Challenging
Group Size Max 8 people
Altitude 2,200 - 2,600m

What's Included

  • Gorilla trekking permit
  • Expert ranger guide
  • Park entrance fees
  • Packed lunch & water
  • Walking stick & porter (optional)
  • Certificate of participation
From $800 per person (permit included)

Primate Adventure

Golden Monkey Tracking

Discover the playful and endangered golden monkeys in the bamboo zones of Mgahinga Gorilla National Park. These beautiful primates, with their striking golden-orange patches, are found only in the Virunga Mountains. Watch them leap through bamboo forests, play in family groups, and feed on bamboo shoots.

This experience is often combined with gorilla trekking for the ultimate primate safari. It's also an excellent alternative for those seeking a less strenuous trek.

Duration 2-4 hours
Difficulty Easy to Moderate
Group Size Max 12 people
Viewing Time 1 hour with monkeys

What's Included

  • Golden monkey tracking permit
  • Expert ranger guide
  • Park entrance fees
  • Refreshments
From $100 per person

Extended Experience

Chimpanzee Habituation

Join researchers in the chimpanzee habituation experience in Kibale Forest, home to over 1,500 chimpanzees - the highest density in Uganda. Unlike regular tracking, habituation allows you to spend up to 4 hours with a chimp community, observing their complex social behaviors, tool use, and hunting strategies.

This is an extraordinary opportunity for wildlife enthusiasts and photographers who want a deeper, more intimate experience with our closest relatives in the animal kingdom.

Duration Full day (6-8 hours)
Difficulty Moderate
Group Size Max 4 people
Viewing Time Up to 4 hours

What's Included

  • Habituation experience permit
  • Expert researcher guide
  • Forest lunch
  • Transportation from lodge
From $250 per person

Adventure Trek

Virunga Volcano Hiking

Climb the majestic volcanoes of the Virunga range - a chain of eight volcanic mountains stretching across Uganda, Rwanda, and DR Congo. Choose from Mount Muhabura (4,127m), Mount Gahinga (3,474m), or Mount Sabyinyo (3,669m) for breathtaking views across three countries.

These hikes traverse diverse ecosystems from bamboo forests to alpine meadows, with opportunities to spot buffalo, forest elephants, and various primate species along the way.

Duration 6-10 hours
Difficulty Challenging
Elevation 3,474m - 4,127m
Summit Options 3 Peaks Available

What's Included

  • Hiking permit
  • Armed ranger guide
  • Packed lunch & water
  • Porter service available
From $100 per person

Birder's Paradise

Bird Watching Safari

With over 350 bird species, including 23 Albertine Rift endemics, Kisoro and the surrounding areas are a birder's paradise. Spot the stunning Rwenzori Turaco, Handsome Francolin, African Green Broadbill, Shelley's Crimsonwing, and many more rare species found nowhere else on Earth.

Our expert birding guides know exactly where to find the most elusive species. Whether you're a serious twitcher or a casual nature lover, our birding safaris cater to all levels of interest.

Duration Half or Full Day
Difficulty Easy
Equipment Provided
Species 350+ Possible

  • Refreshments
