Our Location

Travel

Getting Here

By Air

From Entebbe (Uganda):
Fly to Kihihi or Kisoro airstrip (1 hour), followed by a short 30-minute drive.

From Kigali (Rwanda):
3-hour scenic drive through Rwanda to Kisoro.

By Road

From Entebbe/Kampala:
8-9 hour drive through beautiful Ugandan countryside, or break the journey in Queen Elizabeth National Park.

From Kigali:
3 hours via Cyanika border crossing.

Help

Frequently Asked
Questions

How do I book gorilla permits?

We handle all permit bookings for our guests. Gorilla permits should be booked at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for peak season (June-September and December-February). Contact us to secure your permits.

What's the best time to visit?

Gorilla trekking is possible year-round. Dry seasons (June-September and December-February) offer easier trekking conditions. Green season (March-May, October-November) has fewer tourists and lower prices.

What should I pack?

We recommend waterproof hiking boots, long pants, long-sleeved shirts, rain jacket, gardening gloves, sunscreen, and insect repellent. We provide detailed packing lists upon booking.

Is it safe to visit Uganda?

Yes! Uganda is one of Africa's safest destinations. The areas we visit have excellent security, and our guides are highly experienced. We monitor conditions constantly and prioritize guest safety.

Do I need vaccinations?

Yellow fever vaccination is required for entry. We recommend consulting your doctor about malaria prophylaxis and other travel vaccines at least 6 weeks before travel.

What's included in the package prices?

All packages include accommodation, meals, permits, transfers, and guide services. International flights, travel insurance, tips, and personal expenses are not included. See individual packages for full details.
