Grand Uganda Explorer

The ultimate Uganda adventure for those who want to experience everything this incredible country has to offer. From gorillas to volcanoes, crater lakes to cultural encounters - this is the journey of a lifetime.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1
Arrival & Welcome

VIP airport pickup, luxury transfer, welcome ceremony, sunset drinks on private terrace.

Day 2
First Gorilla Trek

Gorilla trekking in Bwindi, afternoon spa, private candlelit dinner.

Day 3
Virunga Volcano Hike

Summit Mount Sabyinyo (3,669m), three-country viewpoint, celebration at lodge.

Day 4
Lake Mutanda & Culture

Full day on Lake Mutanda - kayaking, island visit, lakeside lunch, Batwa trail experience.

Day 5
Second Gorilla Trek

Second gorilla family encounter, afternoon birding walk, bush dinner.

Lake Bunyonyi Excursion

Day trip to Lake Bunyonyi, dugout canoe experience, community visit, farewell dinner.

Departure

Leisurely morning, final spa treatment, private transfer to airport.
