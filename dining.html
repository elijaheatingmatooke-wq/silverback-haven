Culinary Excellence

Flavors of the
African Highlands

At Silverback Haven, dining is an experience to be savored. Our talented chefs combine locally-sourced ingredients with culinary traditions from across Uganda and East Africa, creating dishes that celebrate the rich flavors of this extraordinary region. From elegant fine dining to intimate bush dinners under the stars, every meal tells a story.

Signature Restaurant

The Mist Restaurant

Our signature restaurant offers a fusion of traditional Ugandan flavors with contemporary African cuisine. Floor-to-ceiling windows provide stunning views of the misty Virunga Mountains as you dine, while our chefs prepare dishes using the freshest local ingredients from surrounding communities.

The menu changes seasonally to reflect the best produce available, with highlights including locally-sourced tilapia from Lake Mutanda, organic vegetables from highland farms, and traditional Ugandan staples reimagined for the modern palate.

Hours Breakfast: 6:30 - 10:00 AM
Lunch: 12:00 - 3:00 PM
Dinner: 7:00 - 10:00 PM
Cuisine Contemporary African

Cocktails & Relaxation

Canopy Bar & Lounge

Unwind after your gorilla trekking adventure at the Canopy Bar & Lounge. Our mixologists craft signature cocktails using local ingredients like Uganda waragi, passion fruit, and wild honey, while you watch the sunset paint the Virunga peaks in golden hues.

The relaxed atmosphere is perfect for sharing stories of the day's adventures with fellow travelers, or simply savoring a quiet moment with a glass of South African wine from our curated selection.

Hours 4:00 PM - Late
Specialty Craft Cocktails & Local Spirits
Ambiance Live acoustic music on weekends

Signature Drinks

  • Silverback Sunset (passion fruit & waragi)
  • Mountain Mist Mojito (wild honey & mint)
  • Virunga Volcano (smoked whiskey blend)
  • Golden Monkey (banana & coffee liqueur)
Exclusive Experience

Bush Dining Experience

Dine under the African stars in an exclusive lakeside setting near Lake Mutanda. Our bush dining experience is the ultimate romantic dinner, featuring a private chef preparing a multi-course menu just for you, surrounded by flickering lanterns and the sounds of the African night.

Intimate Celebrations

Private Dining

Whether celebrating a special occasion or simply seeking an intimate dining experience, our private dining options offer exclusivity and personalized service. Choose from our elegant private dining room in The Mist Restaurant or in-villa dining in your Silverback Villa.

Work with our executive chef to create a custom menu tailored to your preferences, dietary requirements, and the occasion. We specialize in honeymoon dinners, anniversary celebrations, and family gatherings.
